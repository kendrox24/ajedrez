About Me

Chess lessons with an Expert Master! If you want to improve your chess skills or learn from scratch, I am here to help you! I am a certified FSI instructor with years of experience teaching players of all ages and levels. What I offer: Personalized lessons: I tailor my teaching to your needs and skill level. Strategies and tactics: You will learn to plan your moves, recognize patterns, and develop winning strategies. Openings and endgames: We will explore the most common openings and refine your endgame skills. Game analysis: We will study famous games and analyze your games to improve. Flexible schedules: Lessons available both online and in-person. First lesson free to get to know each other and assess your needs. Contact me now to book your first lesson!
